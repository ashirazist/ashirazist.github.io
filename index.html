I am a third year PhD student at Iowa State University in the Department of Statistics. I am currently working with Peng Liu on statistical data analysis of big data and specifically applying integrated data analysis on -Omics data using heterogenous network based and Bayesian based methods. I like to work on big data sets and utilize statistical approaches to reduce the dimension of the data.

I'm interested in the intersection of Statistics and Genetics, and in general statistical Genomics with a penchant for statistical learning and reproducibility. I like learning new languages, R being my first love.

I am also fascinated by high dimensional data sets cleaning utilizing SQL and R, and that is why I am currently learning SQL, Scala and Spark
I completed a master in statistics at Shahid Beheshti University University working with Hamideh D. Hamedani. I received my BS again in statistics at IKIU with Afshin Fallah.

I am an executive member of STATCOM at Iowa State, and currently helping Ames Public Library with a big survey project. I am also a social committee member of STATers (statistical student association) at Iowa State and communicate with many under/graduate students daily, and the former treasurer of ISSA(Iranian Students and Scholars' Assostiation) at Iowa State university with great experience of working in teams and organizing big events.
I enjoy weight lifting, swimming, and I am a soccer lover. My favorite team is Liverpool FC.

PetFindr


PetFindr provides an R interface for the Petfinder.com API (V2). Once a user obtains an API key and secret from Petfinder, this package allows the user to retrieve information about animals by type, breed, location, and other useful characteristics. This package was developed in a team of the five of us at Iowa State University as a part of STAT 585 project. Find more documentation here
